ZACHARIJ MARTIN-NED

"Hello - my name is Zacharij Martin-Ned. I am a 27 year old creative & business owner, originally born in Conroe, Texas. As I began to mature, I realized there was a void of many of these activities in Central Texas (Predominantly Killeen/Copperas Cove/Harker Heights area) I felt we were lacking in the arts, music, expression - the creative side of life.
